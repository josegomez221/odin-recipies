Serve the moist, tender fish with a simple green salad, rice, and a crusty loaf of bread.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2vpounds salmon fillets
  • lemon pepper to taste
  • garlic powder to taste
  • salt to taste
  • 1/3 cup soy sauce
  • 1/3 cup brown sugar
  • 1/3 cup water
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Season

    Season salmon fillets with lemon pepper, garlic powder, and salt.

  2. Marinade

    In a small bowl, stir together soy sauce, brown sugar, wather, and vegetable oil until sugar is dissolved. Place fish in a large resealable plastic bag eith the soy sauce mixture, sal and turn to coat. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours.

  3. Prehead

    Preheat grill for medium heat.

  4. Grill

    Lightly oil grill grate. Place salmon on the preheated grill, and dicard marinade. Cook salmon for 6 to 8 minutes per side, or until the fish flakes easily with a fork.
