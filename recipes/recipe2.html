Garden Fresh Tomato Soup

Food photo created by KamranAydinov - www.freepik.com

Description

A quick and easy recipe for real homemade tomato soup like no other you've had before.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups chopped fres tomatoes
  • 1 slice onion
  • 4 whole cloves
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 2 tablespoons buter
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoons white sugar, or to taste

Steps

  1. In a stock, over medium heat, combine the tomatoes, onion, cloves and chicken broth. Bring to a boil, and gentlu for about 20 minutes to blend all of the flavors. Remove from heat and run the mixture through a food mill into a large bowl, or pan. Discard any stuff left over in the food mill.
  2. In the now empty stockpot, melt the butter over medium heat stir in the flour to make a roux, cooking until the roux is a medium brown. Gradually whisk in a bit of the tomato mixture, so that no lump form, then sitr in the rest, Season with sugar and salt, and adjust to taste.
